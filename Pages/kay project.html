About Kay Stabile

I'm a passionate digital illustrator with over 8 years of experience creating captivating visuals for games, books, and advertising. My work blends traditional artistic principles with modern digital techniques to create pieces that resonate with audiences.

Specializing in character design and world-building, I help clients bring their imaginative concepts to life with vibrant colors, dynamic compositions, and attention to detail that makes each piece tell its own story.

My Creative Process

Every project follows a structured approach to ensure quality and client satisfaction.

1

Concept & Research

Understanding the project vision, gathering references, and developing initial concepts.

2

Sketching

Creating rough sketches to explore composition, perspective, and key elements.

3

Refinement

Developing the chosen sketch with clean linework, values, and color studies.

4

Final Artwork

Rendering the final piece with attention to lighting, textures, and details.
